    About Us

    Welcome to Shambhavi

    Shambhavi, founded in 1999 in Badlapur, Thane, Mumbai, is a distinguished establishment committed to delivering unparalleled hospitality. Our strategic location at Shop No. 9 Shree Yash Building, Badlapur-421503, near Cottonking Shop, ensures optimal accessibility. Placing patrons at the forefront, we prioritize their needs in every aspect of our operations. For inquiries or assistance, feel free to contact us during our business hours. Choose Shambhavi for an exquisite blend of commitment to excellence and highest standards in hospitality.

    FAQ

    Are you kid-friendly?

    Absolutely! We have a kid's menu available, and our staff is always happy to accommodate families with children.

    Are your ingredients locally sourced or organic?

    We prioritize sourcing fresh, high-quality ingredients for our dishes. While not all of our ingredients are organic, we strive to support local farmers and suppliers whenever possible.

    Do you cater for events?

    Yes, we offer catering services for events of all sizes. Please contact us through our website's Contact Us section to discuss your event requirements.

    Is there parking available near the restaurant?

    Unfortunately, we do not have parking facilities directly available near the restaurant. However, there may be street parking or public parking lots available nearby for your convenience.
